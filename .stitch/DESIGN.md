# OpenDataWorks -- Design System

## Brand Feel
Professional, open-source friendly, intelligent.

## Color Direction
Primary: Teal (#0D9488) -- a technical blue-green that signals trustworthy data infrastructure while keeping the page open and approachable.
Mode: Light  Variant: TONAL_SPOT

## Typography
Headlines: SPACE_GROTESK -- Body: IBM_PLEX_SANS

## Shape
Balanced modern radius with 8px corners for product controls, cards, and CTAs.
